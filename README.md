## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Software & AI Engineer** based in Kampala, Uganda 🇺🇬 — I build full-stack products end-to-end, from database schema to polished UI, with AI woven in where it actually adds value.

- 🚀 Solo founder of **FinAdvisor AI** — an AI-powered financial advisor for African markets
- 🌍 Passionate about building for African contexts: local languages, mobile money, offline-first
- 🤖 Deep in **LLM integrations & RAG pipelines** across multiple providers
- 🏆 Hackathon builder — led an 8-person team building **MamaCare**, a maternal health AI assistant over USSD/SMS/Voice
